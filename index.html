Instill an entrepreneurial mindset

The objective of this Summit is essentially to further instill an entrepreneurial and futuristic mindset amongst the African youth community in Malaysia.

Highlighting significance of Social Finance

The Summit is also focused on engaging with the said youth community through highlighting the significanceof Social Finance and identifying investment opportunities

understanding of the working world

In addition, it aims to give the respective participants a better understanding of the working world (and financial market).
